About who we are

CharactersNJ is your central party headquarters founded in New Jersey! We provide family fun entertainment using characters that your children idolize and love. We specialize in putting smiles on everyone's faces from children to even adults when our characters come to visit. Our well trained performers know exactly how to get into character to create a believable experience for your child's party.

There is never a dull moment when you book from CharactersNJ for your next event. Whether it's your child's birthday party, graduation party, bar/bat mitzvah, holiday celebration or community event, CharactersNJ is ready for all types of celebrations.

The party comes to you! CharactersNJ travels throughout the tri-state area offering a wide variety of entertainment that can accommodate any event.
